Information technology management

Also sometime known as information systems

What happens in information technology management?

  • Determining business requirements for it systems
  • Managing it budgets and costs
  • Monitoring safety and compliance
  • Controlling system and network security
  • Implemented new software hardware and data systems
  • Providing technical or help desk support

So what does all this do?

Determines Information Technology strategies and goals for the business and ensure they are implemented.

Processes, Software Hardware, and Systems using a business environment to conduct day to day operations.

Advantages of Information technology management

  • There are very few business processes that do not depend or cannot benefit from information technology
  • Information technology management allows companies to manage their data efficiently regardless of its form
  • Information technology management allow employees to be more productive but it can also be tailor to suit a company's specific needs

Information technology management have several career paths as it has best of both worlds that is business and information technology and it requires strong analytical and mathematical problem solving skills

  1. Project management
  2. Data security and role management
  3. Data organisation and analysis
  4. System analysis design and Organisation
  5. Software and Hardware assessment

Important points:
  • Workers in information technology management are considered part of skilled workforce and sometimes referred to as knowledge workers
  • In addition to typical information technology skill set a career in information technology management requires strong interpersonal skills, management ability and collaborative problem solving skills

Common roles in information technology management

  • Software developers
  • Database & system administrators
  • Information Security analyst
